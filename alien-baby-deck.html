How to make 8 people feel like 80.

Every person in an 8-person agency does 3 jobs.

The strategist is also the researcher. The account lead is also the project manager. The founder is also the business development team. The creative is also the deck builder. Everyone is brilliant at one thing and stretched across three.

The problem isn't talent. It's bandwidth. The work that wins business (thinking, relationships, craft) gets squeezed by the work that keeps the lights on (research, admin, formatting, chasing).

What if every person had a team behind them?

The strategist

Stops spending 2 days on competitor research. Gets it in 10 minutes. Walks into pitches with research packs that look like a 20-person planning department produced them.

The account lead

Never writes a status update, timeline, or scope doc from scratch again. Those generate from a conversation. Hours back every week.

The creative

Has a sparring partner at 11pm when the idea isn't landing. A system that knows the brief, the client, the brand voice. Not a chatbot. A configured colleague.

The founder

Gets morning briefings, pipeline intelligence, and meeting prep without an EA or a BD hire. Walks into every meeting better prepared than the other side.

This isn't AI doing your job. It's AI doing the 60% of your job that isn't creative thinking, so you can do the 40% that wins business.
